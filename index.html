Ant

This was my first major project in Rust, making use of Raylib for drawing and input. It adapts Langton's Ant by allowing the ants to move in 4 additional diagonal directions. To prevent the ants from wandering off endlessly, they wrap around the board and keep interacting with the pheromone trails, which leads to some interesting patterns.
