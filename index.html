Projects

Ant

This was my first major project in Rust, making use of Raylib for drawing and input. It adapts Langton's Ant by allowing the ants to move in 4 additional diagonal directions. To prevent the ants from wandering off endlessly, they wrap around the board and keep interacting with the pheromone trails, which leads to some interesting patterns.

View project

Xtest

This was my first big project for XNL, a Minecraft team that made programs in Lua. I created this to test our programs, and as a general piece of software to be distributed to other players.

It was designed as an alternative to LuaUnit, which would run tests at random. Xtest addressed this by storing each test in a list and running each in order.

Sleep Keeper In Progress!

No more runaway sleep debt! With Sleep Keeper you track the amount of sleep you get for each day in a calendar that shows you the approximate quality of your sleep. Simply enter the amount of hours you slept, along with what time, and receive instant feedback on your sleep habits.
